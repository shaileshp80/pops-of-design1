About

I offer a complete design service where clients can tweak designs. Visuals are given during the creative process. Whether you are looking for a logo refresh or a completely new design, I can create a professional and unique design for you.

I specialize in clothing prints, logo design, flyers, and business cards. I print clothing myself but outsource flyer and business card prints using a quality company with fast delivery and very competitive rates.
